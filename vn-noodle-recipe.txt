Ingredients:
- 1 package Hao Hao noodle
- 1 egg
- 200 ml water

Directions:
- Boiling 200 ml water.
- Put the noodle in a bowl, then put on all spices.
- Fry  the egg, then put it onto the noodle.
- Until water is boiled, pour water into the bowl.
- Close the bowl, wait for 5 minutes.
- Finally, have a meal.
